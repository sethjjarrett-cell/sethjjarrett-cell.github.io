Region 03

Australia

Four months living and working in Melbourne, then the east coast and Sydney. Cool, sunny, and the place that taught me most about integrating quickly into a team where you know nobody.

Stop 01

Melbourne

Spent most of the Australian chapter here, working in a multi-level venue in the city. Eight hours on your feet in a busy bar is its own education in people, pressure, and how to integrate into a team where you know nobody.

Melbourne skyline
The venue
Laneways

Melbourne itself rewards actually living in it rather than passing through. The coffee culture, the laneways, the sport, the food. It has a texture that takes a few weeks to find.

Working in hospitality
Multi-level venue in the city centre. The best crash course in communication and composure under pressure.
The laneways
Street art, coffee, hidden bars. The part of Melbourne that takes a few weeks to find.
The culture
AFL, food markets, art galleries. A city that takes its culture seriously in the best way.

Stop 02

The East Coast

The classic route from Melbourne north to Sydney -- classic for good reason. The landscape shifts constantly and Australia is beautiful in a way that takes you by surprise if you have only seen it in photos.

Great Ocean Road
East coast

Long stretches of coast, small towns, places that feel genuinely remote despite being on the main highway. The Great Ocean Road is one of the better drives anywhere.

The Great Ocean Road
Cliffs, waves, the Twelve Apostles. One of the better drives in the world.
East coast road trip
North from Melbourne. Long stretches of coast, small towns, places that feel genuinely remote.
Byron Bay area
The north end of New South Wales. A different pace from everything south of it.

Stop 03

Sydney

Everything the postcard suggests and then some. The harbour, the Opera House, Bondi -- a city that knows what it is and makes no apologies for it.

Sydney Harbour
Opera House

Warmer than Melbourne, more obviously beautiful. The harbour is one of the great city settings in the world -- it earns every photograph taken of it.

Sydney Harbour
The Bridge, the Opera House, the water. Earns every photograph.
Bondi
The beach everyone talks about. Still worth it.
The ferry network
See the harbour properly by taking the ferries rather than walking everywhere.
