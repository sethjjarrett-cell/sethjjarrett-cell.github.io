I'm a graduate electrical engineer at RED Engineering Design in London, working on large-scale data centre and power infrastructure projects. I hold a First Class MEng from the University of Bristol.

At university I designed and helped build a solar DC microgrid for a remote community in the Brazilian Amazon, and independently completed a wind-solar mini-grid feasibility study awarded 83% and used as an exemplar paper.

I grew up in the Cotswolds, spent two years travelling before starting work, and still think the best things come from getting outside and paying attention to the world around you.
