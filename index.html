Engineer by training,
curious by nature

I'm a graduate electrical engineer at RED Engineering Design in London, working on large-scale data centre and power infrastructure projects. I hold a First Class MEng from the University of Bristol, specialising in energy and renewables.

At university I designed and helped build a solar DC microgrid for a remote community in the Brazilian Amazon, and independently completed a wind-solar mini-grid feasibility study awarded 83% and used as a cohort exemplar.

I grew up in the West Country, spent two years travelling before starting work, picking up experiences along the way -- working at dog shelters, horse races, festivals, and most notably Glastonbury -- and came to believe the best things come from getting outside, trying things that scare you a bit, and paying attention to the world around you.
