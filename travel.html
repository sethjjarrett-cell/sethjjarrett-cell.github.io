What it did

More than a gap year

Two years travelling gave me a real appreciation for how differently people live — the varying standards of living, the cultural contrasts, and the challenges communities face that often go unseen from home.

It left me with a genuine drive to do work that has impact beyond a project deliverable, and pointed me towards international engineering as the direction I want my career to go.
